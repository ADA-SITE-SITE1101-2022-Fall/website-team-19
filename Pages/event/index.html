Hour Of Code

Team 19 (Galaxy Achievers)

Hello everyone. As students of #ADAUniversity and participants of Group number 19 (Farrukh Mammad, Aysu Azammadova, , Ilgar Malikov, Daniz Zeynalova), we held a #HourOfCode event on November 15th at 1:20 p.m. The participants of the event were 24 students of the 8th-grade of Educational Complex No. 132-134. After giving a brief introduction about #ADAUniversity, we informed them about #HourOfCode. #HourOfCode, organized in 2013, is a campaign designed to confirm that it is possible to teach a person the basics of coding within 1 hour, so we tried to teach the students the basics of visual coding through the Code.org website. Later, students completed several code games from Code.org. In the end, the students were awarded certificates for their great participation in the event. We express our gratitude to the director of the school, Ms. Sara Khanlarova, for allowing the event to take place and to the deputry-director, Ms. Ilaha Hasanli, for her support! We thank everyone who participated in our event, especially Mr. Aladdin Shixzamanov!
